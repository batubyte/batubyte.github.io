Teaching 2: Bad Habits and Smoking

Student Awareness Project

Why Smoking is Bad

  • Smoking hurts your lungs.
  • It is very hard to stop smoking.
  • It can give you heart problems.
  • It is bad for young people and their health.

How to Stop Smoking

  • Join school events like Puff Conference.
  • Call the quit line: ALO 171
  • Do sports and breathing exercises.
  • Use apps on your phone to help you stop.

The Little Prince and "The Drunkard"

In the book The Little Prince by Antoine de Saint-Exup√©ry, there is a man called "The Drunkard." He has a bad habit too.

"Why do you drink?" said the Little Prince.
"To forget," said the man.
"Forget what?"
"To forget I am ashamed."
"Ashamed of what?"
"Ashamed because I drink."

This is a cycle. It is like smoking. People feel bad, then smoke again. We must break this cycle.

Positive Habits for a Healthy Life

We learn that addictions can have bad consequences. But we can choose good habits. These habits help us feel better and stay strong.

  • Do sports or exercise.
  • Draw, paint, or make music.
  • Write in a journal. (Devin)
  • Try mindfulness or deep breathing.

We will make fun workshops at school. Students can try these activities and listen to others. We will also make a board at school with tips and stories. The school counselor will help us too.
